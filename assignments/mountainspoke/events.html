Cycle the Continental Divide

Three full days of mountain biking in Zion's Peak, Utah will leave you both exhausted and replenished. Giant old growth trees, lush ferns, creeks, waterfalls, and mountain meadows full of wildflowers make for some of the most beautiful singletrack around. From rolling riverside trails, to wild ripping descents, The Mountain Smoke has something for everyone.

With its small festival atmosphere, The Mountain Smoke is a unique experience. Join us for the most fun mountain bike weekend you'll ever have! Each day offers supported ride options for intermediate and advanced riders (this is not a beginner event).
